Project Overview

Title: Menstrual Cycle Research Study.

Role & Responsibilities: UX Researcher, responsible for crafting consent forms, recruiting participants, and conducting 5 interviews to gain more information about my objectives.

Tools: Zoom, Apple's Voice Memos, Adobe Acrobat, and Google Docs.


Process & Approach

Context: In class, we were given the objective of trying to understand how, or if, AI can be used to help make a specific process easier; this "specific process" would be my chosen sub-objective.

Research Objective: To understand how women who struggle with their periods manage it and locate resources and information.


Most Important Findings

Most participants have privacy concerns about the use of AI to help them track and monitor their cycles. Some participants, however, see it as a convenient way to obtain information about their cycles.

Participants also expressed negative healthcare experiences that could've been improved by more attention, care, and clearer information from the healthcare provider.
